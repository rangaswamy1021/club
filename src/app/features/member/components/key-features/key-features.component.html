Key Features

These are the membership key points and features.

Membership validity

Membership is valid for 8 years for the fee of Rs 20000 Plus GST.

Membership EMI

Membership can be converted to EMI with zero interest rate.

Maintenance Fee

Maintenance fee of Rs 1000 per year from 3rd year of registration.

Membership Transfer

Membership can be transferred with after 18 months with a minimum cost of Rs 1000 plus GST..

Each Member Eligible

Each member is eligible for 6 nights per year on 2 nights booking basis.

Membership Gift

Membership days can be Gifted.

Booking Gap

There should be a gap of 45 days for each booing and need to book his slot minimum 15 days prior to travel.

Membership South Sates

Membership covers majorly south sates of India. Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Goa

Member Exit

Exit option to customers after 18 months of registration.
